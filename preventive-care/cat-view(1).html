Comprehensive Doorstep Care for Cats

At petzz we believe in providing the best care for your pets. A major part of this care, we believe, can and should be delivered at your home where your pets feel safe. Hence, we have created a network of cat vets and other feline professionals to cater to your pets directly at your doorstep. Our experts come from diverse backgrounds and expertise in treatment of a wide spectrum of cat breeds and species. We believe in prognosis rather than diagnosis and believe that your pets should not be allowed to fall sick in the first place. Hence, we have instituted special healthcare plans which ensure regular preventive health check-ups directly at your home. Apart from health check-ups our cat doctors can also deliver core services like cat vaccinations, cat deworming tablet administration. We also have a team of expert cat groomers who can take care of the sanitary needs of your beloved cats at your home at a time of your choosing. We know that not all the treatments can be done at home, especially those involving surgical intervention. Hence, our vet network also consists of cat clinics and cat hospitals who expertise in surgical and higher tier treatments. It is our aim to provide the best care for your pets and the complete system is geared towards it.
